Help Center

Need guidance or support? We’ve got you covered. Learn how to use YourWorkBuddy effectively and get quick answers to your questions.

Create your account, explore available services, and start using the platform with ease. Whether you're seeking help or offering your skills, onboarding is intuitive and quick.

  • How to post a job: Go to dashboard → "Post a Job", fill in job details.
  • Managing applicants: View/manage candidates under “My Jobs”.
  • Hiring workers: Use messaging to confirm details and finalize hiring.

  • How to search for jobs: Use filters to browse relevant listings.
  • Setting job preferences: Update your profile to match your skills and availability.
  • Tracking applications: Monitor progress under “My Applications”.

  • How to reset your password? Use “Forgot password” on login page.
  • How to update your profile? Go to "Edit Profile" from the dashboard.
  • How to contact support? Use the About Us form or email us directly.
